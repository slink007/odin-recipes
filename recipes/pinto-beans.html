Pinto Beans

Description

Southern style pinto beans.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried pinto beans
  • 4 quarts of water
  • 4 (1 ounce) slices of fatback

Steps

  1. Wash pinto beans thoroughly and place in a large stockpot.
  2. Add all ingredients.
  3. Bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook for 3 to 4 hours. Add water throughout cooking as needed to prevent beans from going dry.
