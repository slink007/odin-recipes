Banana Pudding

Description

Easy version of banana pudding

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) container of frozen whipped topping (thawed)
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of vanilla wafers
  • 1/4 cup melted, unsalted butter
  • 3 or 4 sliced bananas

Steps

  1. Place milk and pudding mix into a large bowl. Beat with a whisk until smooth.
  2. Stir in vanilla, then fold in whipped topping.
  3. Layer wafers, banana slices, and pudding in a separate container.
  4. Chill and serve.
